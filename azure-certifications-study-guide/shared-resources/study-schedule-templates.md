## üí° Study Tips

### Before Starting

| Tip | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Set a date | Schedule your exam |
| Create environment | Dedicated study space |
| Gather resources | All materials ready |
| Tell someone | Accountability partner |

### During Study

| Tip | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Active learning | Take notes, don't just watch |
| Hands-on practice | Do the labs, not just read |
| Spaced repetition | Review previous topics |
| Practice tests | Take frequently |

### Before Exam

| Tip | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Final review | Focus on weak areas |
| Rest well | Sleep is important |
| Light study | Day before, minimal |
| Logistics | Know exam location/setup |

## üîÑ Adjusting Your Schedule

### Signs You Need to Adjust

| Sign | Adjustment |
|------|------------|
| Falling behind | Reduce other commitments |
| Topics too hard | Spend more time, find resources |
| Too easy | Speed up, add depth |
| Burnout | Take breaks, reduce hours |

### Common Adjustments

| Situation | Solution |
|-----------|----------|
| Work is busy | Switch to extended schedule |
| Unexpected free time | Switch to intensive schedule |
| Struggling with topic | Add extra week |
| Already expert | Skip familiar content |
